Day Trips

Corolla Wild Horse Tours

Corolla Wild Horse Tours knowledgeable guides will ensure you and your family have a great wild horse tour! Along the way the guides will stop and point out interesting sites and local history and details of the Corolla Wild Horses history.

Currituck Beach Light Station

Since 1875, this lighthouse has kept mariners from danger, night and day. The Keepers’ Dwelling has housed more than two dozen families, as their duties of “Lighthouse Keeper” were carried out. Today, our on-site keepers and staff continue the tradition: cleaning the lens, maintaining the structure and grounds, and most importantly, now – sharing it with you.

Corolla & Duck Parasail

Soar high at 500 feet or 800 feet of line (the max allowable height as determined by the FAA) for stunning views of the area. Parasailing on the Outer Banks is one of the most sought-after kid-friendly and family-friendly activities, so call or book online now to enjoy unmatched panoramic view of these incredible barrier islands with Corolla & Duck Parasail—the Outer Banks’ leading and ONLY parasail

Pirates Island Golf and Arcade

Pirate-Themed 18-Hole Minature Golf Course and Arcade with waterfalls, a pirate ship, caves, skulls, and more! So much fun for the whole family! Come to Corolla Light's premier attraction located in Corolla Light Town Center. Your family will love this adventure!

Restaurants

Uncle Ike's Sandbar & Grill

ARE YOU HUNGRY? Uncle Ike's full menu is served in both the dining room and our bar, offering lunch and dinner, seven days a week, from mid-May to September with plenty of off-season hours. In both the dining room and the bar, we offer a variety of tasty starters, salads, sandwiches and delicious creative entrees to please all palates. The inspiration for our diverse menu items is comfort food done really well. We add a healthy Outer Banks beach flair, which makes Uncle Ike's a "must" for first-time visitors and repeat vacationers alike. We also offer steamed bar, vegetarian, kid-friendly and gluten-free options. At Uncle Ike's, everything is fresh, and made to order, so, please be patient. Good things take time!

Corolla Pizza Deli

Corolla Pizza has become a staple in the Northern Outer Banks, serving generations of vacationers and local Outer Bankers since 1989! People return year after year for our extra-large slices, gigantic strombolis, yummy cheesesteaks, and our fresh, hand-tossed pizzas.
